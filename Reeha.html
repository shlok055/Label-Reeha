ASSAM
IN EVERY
THREAD

At Label Reeha, every piece of jewellery is more than an accessory—it’s a handcrafted story. Each design is meticulously created using traditional Assamese textiles. Assam’s rich heritage in weaving, especially Assam Silk and the renowned Assam Muga, is celebrated worldwide for its beauty, vibrancy, and intricate motifs.

Inspired by this cultural richness, we envisioned jewellery that blends these exquisite textiles with timeless, hand-crafted artistry. The result is a collection where age-old techniques meet contemporary, bohemian charm—pieces that resonates with customers of all ages and style preference.

Explore Philosophy
"Label Reeha is a manifestation of a lifelong dream. My journey began in the jewellery industry, where I cultivated not just a career, but a genuine passion for craftsmanship. Over time, the desire to build my own brand—one rooted in tradition, identity, and authenticity—grew stronger.
Two things remained unwavering throughout this journey: First, my designs would be deeply inspired by the heritage of Assam and the Northeast—their timeless textiles, intricate motifs, and the stories woven into their traditions. Second, the name had to reflect a meaningful connection to Assam and its cultural essence.
— Binita Baruah, Founder/Designer
